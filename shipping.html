Shipping & Returns

Fast, secure delivery and hassle-free returns

Shipping Overview

At Soultouch ZA, we understand that getting your premium sneakers quickly and safely is important. We partner with trusted courier services to ensure your orders arrive in perfect condition.

All orders are carefully packaged to protect your sneakers during transit. We use premium packaging materials to maintain the integrity of your purchase.

Shipping Areas

We currently ship nationwide within South Africa. This includes all major cities and towns across all provinces.

  • Gauteng (Johannesburg, Pretoria, and surrounding areas)
  • Western Cape (Cape Town and surrounding areas)
  • KwaZulu-Natal (Durban and surrounding areas)
  • Eastern Cape, Free State, Limpopo, Mpumalanga, Northern Cape, North West

For international shipping inquiries, please contact us.

Delivery Times

Delivery times are calculated from the date your order is confirmed and payment is processed. Processing typically takes 1-2 business days.

Standard Delivery

3-5 business days

Ideal for standard orders. Your package will be delivered to your specified address during business hours.

Express Delivery

1-2 business days

Perfect when you need your sneakers fast. Express orders are prioritized and shipped immediately after processing.

Please note: Delivery times are estimates and may vary based on location, public holidays, and courier availability. Rural areas may experience slightly longer delivery times.

Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your delivery method and location.

  • Standard Delivery: R100 (nationwide)
  • Express Delivery: R180 (nationwide)

Free shipping may be available for orders over a certain amount. Check our promotions page for current offers.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order has been shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your package in real-time.

Visit our Order Tracking page to check the status of your order at any time.

If you have any concerns about your shipment, please contact our support team.

Returns Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we offer a straightforward returns process.

Return Window

You have 14 days from the date of delivery to initiate a return.

Return Conditions

To be eligible for a return, items must:

  • Be in their original, unworn condition
  • Include all original packaging, tags, and accessories
  • Not show any signs of wear, damage, or alteration
  • Be in the same condition as when received

How to Request a Return

  1. Contact us via email or our Contact Us page within 14 days of delivery
  2. Provide your order number and reason for return
  3. We'll send you a return authorization and shipping label
  4. Package the item securely in its original packaging
  5. Ship the item back to us using the provided label

Refund Processing

Once we receive and inspect your returned item, we'll process your refund within 5-10 business days. Refunds will be issued to your original payment method.

Please note: Original shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error or a defective product.

Exchanges

We currently do not offer direct exchanges. If you need a different size or style, please return the original item and place a new order.

This ensures you get the exact product you want and helps us maintain our inventory accuracy.

If you need assistance selecting the right size, please contact us before placing your order.

Questions?

If you have any questions about shipping or returns, our customer service team is here to help. Get in touch and we'll respond as soon as possible.
